On the Main Menu enter two names for the players for this game. Player One wil be assigned X's and Player 2 will be assigned O's.

1. Press "Start Game" and the game board will appear on the screen. There are nine squares arranged in a 3x3 grid.
2. Player 1 starts by clicking on an empty square on the game board. The square will then be filled with an "X".
3. Player 2 takes their turn by clicking on an empty square on the game board. The square will be filled with an "O".
4. Players continue taking turns until one of them gets three of their symbols in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

If a player gets three of their symbols in a row, they win the game!
If all of the squares on the game board are filled and no player has won, the game is a draw.
Each time someone wins it will be added to the scoreboard until the game is reset.

May the best player win!
